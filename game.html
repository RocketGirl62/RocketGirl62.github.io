Game: Gems of Knowledge

Foxy is a brown among white arctic foxes. In his everyday life he is exposed to racist statements, attacks and prejudices. He is mocked and partly marginalized. Foxy needs your help to educate the residents of his village to fight against racism.

In order to help him, of course, you have to be familiar with the subject of racism. On this website you will find the information you need. With our two quizzes you can proof your skills. For each correctly answered quiz you will receive a part of the code with which you can unlock the game against racism "Gems of knowledge" to help Foxy.

Good luck and have fun!
