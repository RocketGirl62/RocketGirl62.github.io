What can we do against racism?

A big question when people talk about racism is what can we do against it?

You may first think of the “Black Lives Matter” movement. “Black Lives Matter” is a movement, which started in the USA when people particularly young people where protesting against violence of police brutality racially motivated against black people. Actually, it started in May 2020 because the US-American George Floyd died because of police violence. The reaction from the community were protests against this kind of violent procedure of the police. But it didn’t stop in Minneapolis where George Floyd died. All over the world people started to protest against police violence and racism. Movements like this help a lot to raise awareness on racism, because they are getting a lot of attention in different media and make people think about their own behaviors.

Global movements like this help to prevent racism. But you can also do something in your community or school. The most important thing when you want to prevent racism is to raise awareness. Only when one knows what racism is, one can think about their own behavior and change it. It’s necessary to inform people for example via presentations or websites to raise their awareness. Everyone can do something against racism. Even if only one person stops being racist because of the action you took it still helps to improve the overall situation. Even a small step is still a step in the right direction.

Remember:

Everyone can do something against racism. The most important thing is to raise awareness. Not just huge movements like “Black Lives Matter” can lead to change. Also, presentations or websites can help on it.
