What is racism?

Racism means the belief that humans are dived in different groups because of biology and therefor the discrimination of some groups. Most people when they hear racism think about the discrimination of people because of their origin or skin color but there mostly is also a connection to the religion of these people.

The start of racism was because of the attempt to find an answer to the question why people from Africa are different to people from Europe. They were trying to find an explanation why white people are worth more than black people. In this time the scientist Charles Darwin discovered that there are different races of for example birds. Some people have misinterpreted this, and racism was born.

Of course, now its scientifically proven that there´s no difference but it´s still in the heads of some people.
