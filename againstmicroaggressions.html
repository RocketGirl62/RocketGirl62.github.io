What can we do against microaggressions and where do we find them?

You can find microaggressions everywhere. Many people use them without knowing that they could hurt or what they really mean. Even small things you’re saying without thinking about it or intending to hurt someone can be microaggressions. Therefore, it’s very important to be informed about them. You might have heard microaggressions on the streets or at school for example. It’s very common to say things like that but nobody thinks about what this could do with the other person. Of course, there are some people intending to hurt someone, However, the majority doesn’t intend to insult anyone.

As you can see many people don’t intend to discriminate anyone so this is the first point where you can do something against it. To stop people from doing this without knowing it’s necessary to raise awareness. Therefore, it’s possible to inform them with presentations or websites like this one. An also schools can take a big part in raising awareness. To prevent racism and microaggressions schools should inform their students and teachers more so it doesn’t happen accidently that a teacher or students uses a microaggression without intending to hurt the other person.

Remember:

You’ll find microaggressions everywhere but the majority of people aren’t intending to insult anyone.

Everyone can do something to stop microaggression. It’s very important to raise awareness so nobody accidently hurt someone anymore. Presentations, websites, or similar things help a lot.
